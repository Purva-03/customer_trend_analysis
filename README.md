# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Customer Behavior Analysis** using **Python, MySQL, and Power BI**.
The goal is to extract meaningful insights from customer transaction data and support data-driven business decisions.

The project follows a complete analytics pipeline:

* Data Cleaning & Preparation (Python)
* Data Storage & Querying (MySQL)
* Data Visualization & Insights (Power BI)

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Identify high-value customer segments
* Understand product performance and revenue trends
* Detect churn-risk customers
* Provide actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** → Data preprocessing
* **MySQL** → Data storage & SQL analysis
* **Power BI** → Interactive dashboard & visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains customer transaction details such as:

* Customer demographics (age, gender, location)
* Purchase behavior (amount, frequency, category)
* Product details (item, category, rating)
* Order attributes (shipping type, discount applied, season)

---

## ⚙️ Data Pipeline

### 1️⃣ Data Preparation (Python)

* Handled missing values
* Converted data types
* Created new features like:

  * Age groups
  * Purchase metrics

---

### 2️⃣ Data Storage (MySQL)

* Loaded cleaned data into MySQL database

### 3️⃣ Visualization (Power BI)

Developed an interactive dashboard including:

#### 📈 Customer Insights

* Revenue by Gender
* Revenue by Age Group

#### 🛍️ Product Analysis

* Top Selling Products

#### ⚙️ Behavioral Insights

* Discount Impact
* Shipping Type Analysis

#### 📉 Churn Analysis

* Identification of churn-risk customers
* Churn rate calculation

#### 📅 Trends

* Monthly revenue trends
* Seasonal sales patterns

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* A small group of customers contributes a large portion of revenue
* Returning customers play a crucial role in business growth
* Certain product categories dominate sales
* Discounts significantly influence customer purchases
* Specific age groups contribute more to revenue
* Churn-risk customers exist and require retention strategies

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Implement loyalty programs to retain high-value customers
* Optimize discount strategies for better profitability
* Focus marketing efforts on top-performing customer segments
* Ensure stock availability for best-selling products
* Target churn-risk customers with personalized offers
